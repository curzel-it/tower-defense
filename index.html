A squad of heroes against an endless horde.

Shape the maze in the build phase, then hold the line: switch heroes, spend your coins on ammo, weapons and revives, and survive wave after wave on ever-harder maps — solo or in co-op.

Features

  • Build → wave → clear: maze the path, then hold the line, on ever-harder maps.
  • A squad of distinct heroes — switch who you drive; the rest fight as AI allies.
  • Finite ammo + an in-run shop: coins from kills buy ammo, weapons and consumables.
  • Recruit and revive heroes mid-run from a shared coin purse.
  • Local split-screen and online co-op — defend together right in the browser.
  • Keyboard and gamepad support; runs on desktop, mobile, and the web.
